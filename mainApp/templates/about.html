What is SportsInvest?

This site was designed with educational purposes in mind. Users of SportsInvest are encouraged to learn the basics of market trading using familiar commodities. The currency represented in this site is 100% virtual.

Who should use SportsInvest?

From the experienced trader to the casual NBA fan, SportsInvest offers an interactive way to simultaneously root on your favorite sports teams while learning the ins and outs of market trading.

How are teams evaluated?

A team's market price is determined by many factors including winning / losing games, league standing, win streaks and more. Each game a team plays updates trading prices depending on pre-determined win probability.

Can I play with real money?

When you signed up for SportsInvest, your account was granted $500 virtual dollars. At this time, we are not accepting real currency.
